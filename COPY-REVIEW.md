### Problem — "The leak"
- **H2:** Your growth stalled for a reason. You just can't name which one yet.
- Your product ships, customers sign, the deck gets fuller, and then the curve flattens and no one can say exactly why. Sales says the leads are weak. Marketing says the follow-up is slow. You call another all-hands.
- Your leak could sit anywhere: a message the market outgrew, a channel mix that stopped paying back, an onboarding step that quietly loses people, or a retention gap no one owns. Guessing at it burns your quarters. Naming it is the whole game. That's where we'd start.

If you can find a senior one, afford the salary plus equity, and wait three to six months for them to ramp, do it. Most post-PMF teams can't justify that yet. I give you the senior thinking now, on the specific problem that's stalling growth, without the full-time commitment.

**How do we actually work together?**
I embed in your team. Your Slack, your tools, your standups. We start by finding where growth is breaking, agree on the first fix, and I do the work. Hands-on and senior, not a deck thrown over the wall.

**What does it cost?**
It depends on what the work needs, so a number on a webpage wouldn't mean much. Book a call, tell me what's stalled, and I'll give you a straight scope and price.

**What if it's not working out?**
I work month to month. If the fit is wrong or the work is done, you stop. I'd rather have four clients who want to be there than lock anyone in.

**Do you only work with SaaS?**
Mostly B2B SaaS, because that's where twelve years of my reps are. But if you're post-PMF with paying customers and growth that's stalled, the playbook usually travels. Ask me.

**What's the free Growth Report, really?**
A real diagnosis, not a brochure. You answer a few questions about your funnel and get back a scored read on where it's leaking and the likely cause. If you want help fixing it, we talk. If not, the report is yours to keep.
